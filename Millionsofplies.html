Millions of Pliés

Stomach in. Shoulders down.

Welcome to Millions of Pliés! your guide to navigating New York City's Dance scene without going bankrupt (or relying on your parent's life savings to get you through your time here!). Here you can find all sorts of information; like how to find the right pair of pointé shoes, how much you should be paying for a leotard, and even advice for starting your own dance company.

My mission is to prove that regardless of who you are, your background, or your training you can make it as a dancer in New York City!!
